Ingredients:
 - Hot tap water
 - 2 handfuls Frozen Berries
 - 1 serving Vega One Vanilla Chai powder
 - 1 Tbsp Ground Flax seeds
 - Strawberry Kaffir drink

Equipment
 - Magic Bullet blender

Directions:
1. Place frozen berries in magic bullet cup
2. Add hot tap water to fil cupt 1/2 full
3. Wait minimum 5 min for berries to thaw
4. Carefully pour out approx 3/4 of hot water, making sure berries don't fall out
5. Add Vega One power
6. Add Flax Seed
7. Add Kaffir
8. Blend
9. Drink and enjoy
